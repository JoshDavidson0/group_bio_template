Our Mission Statement

Our mission is to create a dependable and supportive team environment built on open communication and respect. We commit to respecting each others time by meeting deadlines, mentioning conflicts early, and maintaining a focused collaboration.

Team Rules

- Each team member will respect each other's time and make sure we are doing our part in a timely manner for the deadline.

- If a member anticipates missing a deadline, they must notify the team as early as possible so adjustments can be made.

- Team members will speak up when they see risks, blockers, or unclear requirements.

- Members will ask for help when they are stuck instead of silently falling behind.

- Meetings will stay on topic and follow an agenda.

- Feedback will be encouraged and handled constructively.

- The team will communicate with transparency about workload, availability, and skill limitations.
